Warranty

We offer a standard 1-year manufacturer's warranty on all our artificial plants. This includes:

  • Warranty against manufacturing defects
  • Warranty against construction (e.g., loose parts)
  • Solution via replacement or repair

Returns & Cooling-off Period

14-day cooling-off period: You have the right to cancel your order up to 14 days after receipt without giving a reason. After cancellation, you have another 14 days to return the product.

Return conditions:

  • Product in original condition and packaging
  • Only unpacked for inspection
  • Not used or damaged

Return costs: At your own expense, unless the product is damaged or incorrectly delivered.

Refund: Within 14 days after registering the return, via the same payment method.

Received a damaged product?

Send us an email with a photo immediately. We will ensure a quick solution or replacement.

Exchange

Place a new order and return the old product. You will receive the purchase amount back once the return is processed.

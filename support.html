Care & Maintenance

Keep your plants looking perfect

01

Regular dusting

Use a soft cloth, feather duster, or compressed air to gently remove dust from leaves and branches. Do this every 2–4 weeks for the best appearance.

02

Deep cleaning

For a thorough refresh, wipe leaves with a slightly damp microfiber cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaning agents.

03

Placement tips

Place your plant away from prolonged direct sunlight to preserve color vibrancy. Our plants are suitable for any indoor environment, including rooms with no natural light.

04

Reshaping

After unpacking or moving, gently fan out branches and adjust leaves to restore the natural, full volume. Most stems are flexible and will hold their shape.

 PlantBrothers: Premium E-Commerce Architecture
System Overview & Handover Document

This document summarizes the high-end technical implementations that make the PlantBrothers checkout flow secure, scalable, and operationally efficient. These features go beyond a "basic shop" and represent a custom-engineered enterprise solution.

🚀 1. Dynamic Shipping Engine (Real-Time)
Instead of using static, restrictive shipping rules in the Stripe Dashboard, we built a Serverless Shipping Engine.

How it works: The moment a customer selects their country in the checkout, our backend instantly calculates the exact shipping fee based on your specific business logic (e.g., Free over €100 for NL, €25 for International).
Benefit: No manual maintenance of Stripe shipping zones. It handles complex region-based pricing (NL, BE/DE, EU, International) flawlessly and updates the total live on-screen.
🛡️ 2. Enterprise-Grade Security (Anti-Tamper)
We implemented a "Source of Truth" security model to prevent common e-commerce hacks.

Anti-Tamper Defense: The system ignores the "Total Price" sent by the user's browser. Instead, our backend retrieves the actual price from the secured Stripe session before calculating shipping or processing the payment. A user cannot "hack" free shipping or lower the price via the browser console.
Duplicate Protection: The system monitors for duplicate payment signals. If a payment event is sent twice (common with network glitches), our logic identifies the duplicate and prevents double-entry in your database.
🎨 3. Seamless Embedded Checkout
We migrated from the old "Redirect-to-Stripe" method to a modern Embedded Flow.

The Experience: Customers never leave plantbrothers.nl. The payment UI is mounted directly into your site, maintaining the premium brand feel and significantly increasing conversion rates.
Branded UI: The checkout box is automatically styled via the Stripe Branding settings, ensuring colors and fonts match the 4EverPlants aesthetic.
📦 4. Packaging-Ready Fulfillment
The system is designed for operational speed.

Clean Data Extraction: Instead of messy notifications, the system generates a structured summary with the address broken down by Country, City, Zip, and Street.
Itemized Orders: Every purchase is broken down into a "Packing List" showing exactly which plants were bought, in what quantity, and with what shipping method.
📊 5. Supabase Order Synchronization
Every successful order is instantly synced to a centralized database.

Order Tracking: All customer details, shipping info, and Stripe Session IDs are stored securely.
Line Item Detail: We don't just store the "Total"; we store every individual item bought, making it easy to track inventory and sales performance over time.
🛠️ Maintenance & Management
Order Status: Manage orders directly in the Supabase Dashboard. Change status from paid to shipped with a single click.
Branding: Update your logo or brand colors anytime in the Stripe Dashboard > Settings > Branding.
Future Ready: This architecture is 100% compatible with future upgrades like automated shipping label printing or a dedicated Customer Account portal.

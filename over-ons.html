Sustainability

Sustainable enjoyment, without concessions

With us you choose high-quality artificial plants that are not only aesthetically attractive, but also a conscious and sustainable choice. Our plants are designed to last for years, without loss of quality or maintenance. So you prevent waste and do not have to purchase plants again and again.

Unlike many real plants from tropical regions, which often require a long journey and are sensitive to climate changes, our artificial plants will always remain in perfect condition. Real plants need regular replacement because they cannot survive the indoor climate — leading to additional transportation, packaging and CO2 emissions.

Our artificial plants offer a future-proof alternative. They last a long time, ensure fewer logistics movements and thus contribute to lower environmental impact. In addition, our plants do not contain plastic, which makes them an even more conscious choice within a sustainable lifestyle.

A smart choice for you and the world

With our artificial plants you combine comfort, style and sustainability. You invest once in a product that will last for years — without waste, without maintenance and with minimal impact on the environment.
